Interactive Food insecurity among children

By Alexis Barnes

Food insecurity is when a person has limited access to adquate food. Not only are individuals experiencing food insecurity, there are households who suffer from it as well. According to the data provided by the USDA, food insecurity is most prevalent in households with children.

The rates of low and very food security have been going up and down since 1995, but it increased about 4% in 2008.

*Low Food Security includes very low food security

How are children affected in California?

Low-income families suffer more from lack of food security.
